CRYSTAL SUN

About Me

This is Crysta Sun.

I believe that data visualization can change the way people interact with and analyze the data by simplifying.

Now I am graduating from Data Science Master program at University of San Francisco. I also hold bachelor degrees in CS and Math at University of Minnesota and BS in Information Security at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. I have been training in the fields of data science, visualizations, computer science, and statistics.

Currently, I am working on developing visualizations for the City of San Francisco using projected data of commuting times, jobs, and population growth. I use Vue.js, D3.js, mapbox.js, and leaflet.js for map implementation and use SQL, PostgREST, and Python to connect front-end with the back-end.
